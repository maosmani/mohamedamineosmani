Asking yourself: how to secure my passwords?



If you are asking yourself this question…

Then today you are going to know the answer for your question…

A lot of people ask themselves the question:

How to secure my passwords?

And it’s a good question…

Your passwords are your first line of defense against hackers…and Cybercriminals…

Because if a hacker gets your passwords…

He can do a lot of bad things to you…

He can steal your money from your bank account…

He can get all your important data…

He can know a lot of sensitive information about you…your work…

For these reasons, you should always take care of your passwords…

If you have a good passwords and you follow the rules for choosing strong passwords…

Then hackers can’t get your passwords...

But how do you choose strong passwords…

Today we‘re going to talk about how to choose a strong passwords…

How to choose strong secure passwords:

Here are a few tips that will help you to choose strong and secure passwords…

Don’t use words as passwords instead always use passphrases:

But what a passphrase?

A passphrase it’s just a group of words that you use to gather as a password…

For example instead of using the password: good

Here in this password, we have used the word good as a password…

So instead of using a single word as a password, we should use many words…

When we use many words it will become a passphrase…

So let’s turn the password good into a passphrase…

For example goodmorningjohn

Now instead of using a single word…

We have used the phrase good morning john as a password…

Use long passwords:

use passwords that are more than 12 characters long…

Because long passwords are so hard to crack…

So if your password is short…

Then a hacker can crack it in a short time…

But if your password is long…

Then a hacker can’t crack it in a short time…

It will take him years even decades to crack your password…

So always make your passwords long…

Always make your password consist of:

Lowercase characters

Uppercase characters

Use number

Use special character

A good password uses all of them…

Because when you use all of them...your password will become harder to crack…

So use all of them in your passwords…

Never reuse the same password on two different sites:

Because if one site gets hacked and a hacker gets your password…

Then he will try to use the same password on a different site…

So never reuse the same password…

Don’t share your passwords with others:

Because they can lose it…

And what will happen if they lose it…

Maybe someone else will get it and he will try to login into your accounts…

So don’t share your passwords…
