how to stay safe online?



Hackers are everywhere they are always spreading new viruses and sending fishing links.

They are looking for new targets that they can hack and get their money and personal information.

So how do you stay safe online?

And because the internet is very dangerous you will need always to protect yourself.

Because if you don’t protect yourself any hacker can hack you.

But what will happen if a hacker gets into your phone or laptop?

He can do a lot of things to your machine.

For example, he can steal your data and get your passwords.

He can open the microphone and listen to you.

He can open the webcam and see you.

He can do anything you can do on your machine.

For these reasons, you should always look for ways to stay safe online.

Here are a few tips that will help you to stay safe online.

Use secure passwords:

Use a secure password because if your password is weak hackers can hack you and login into your accounts.

You should always use a secure password because it will make your accounts stronger.

A secure password is just a password that is long and contains lower case and upper case letters with numbers and special characters.

Always make sure to use the long sentence as your password.

Don’t use your pet name as a password or your name because hackers can guess them.

So use a long sentence as a password and make sure that it contains a mix of lower case and upper case characters with numbers and special characters.

Never use the same password on multiple sites:

Don’t use the same password for multiple sites.

Because if a hacker gets your password he can’t use it on multiple sites.

So always keep in mind that you should use a different password for each site.

Each site you use should have its password.

Use antivirus:

Use antivirus because it will protect you from viruses and it will remove them from your system.

Sometimes hackers use viruses to steal passwords and data so if you use one it will protect you from the common viruses.

Never click on the link:

when you get an email they tell you to click on the link and never click on that link.

But why?

Because hackers send links to their victims and when they click on them they will get hacked.

Sometimes they will send you a link and tell you to login into your Facebook account or your bank account

Never enter your credentials if you get a message or email like this.

Because hackers use this technique all time for hacking their victims.

Install updates:

when companies find a bug in their software or a venerability that allows hackers to hack their users.

They send updates to fix these problems.

So always update your operating system and software to fix bugs and vulnerabilities to prevent hackers from using them to hack your device.

If you want to stay safe online then always use these tips and advice because if you don’t use them hackers can hack you.
